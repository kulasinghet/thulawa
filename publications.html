Thulawa Association

The Thulawa Association, established by the Faculty of Law at University of Colombo, aims to educate citizens on legal knowledge and create a law-abiding society. The Thulawa Law Magazine, an official student publication, was first published in 2016 and aims to provide comprehensive legal knowledge to the public.

Submissions

Articles can be submitted either in English, Sinhala, or Tamil medium, under any field of law.

★ In the Thulawa law journal, the percentage of articles included based on the language they are written in is as follows.

• Articles written in English: 30%

• Articles written in Sinhala and Tamil: 70%

★ Articles co-authored by more than one author can also be submitted, but the maximum number of authors allowed per article is three.

★ All articles must be submitted in MS Word format.

★ The first page of the document must include the title of the article, academic/professional qualifications of the author/s, email address, and phone number (WhatsApp number).

★ When naming the document, at least two parts of the author’s name (first name/middle name and surname) must be used. If the article is co-authored by more than one person, one part of each author's name (first name or middle name) should be used for naming the document.

★ The document must be named as follows: Author’s First Name/ Middle Name and Last Name.

★ Submitted article must be original and it should not have been submitted for publication elsewhere.

★ Articles that contain any indication of plagiarism or that have been written using artificial intelligence will not be accepted.

Review Process

★ Articles will be reviewed through an Anonymous Review process based on the following criteria:

• The contribution made by the article to the development of the relevant legal field.

• Clarity of presentation.

• Precision of the content.

• Technical aspects.

★ The review process and the final decision will be carried out by a review board consisting of lecturers of the Faculty of Law, University of Colombo.

★ All articles must be submitted to the following email address: thulawampuocfol@gmail.com

★ Once your article is submitted, we will confirm receipt via a confirmation email within 48 hours. If you do not receive confirmation within 48 hours, kindly resubmit your article.

Thulawa Law Journal Volume IV 2024

The Thulawa Law Magazine: the official undergraduate publication of the Faculty of Law, University of Colombo, is now calling submissions for its 2024 edition. The primary objective of this magazine is to disseminate legal knowledge to both the law student community and Sri Lankan society at large. By providing a platform for undergraduates to engage with contemporary legal issues, the magazine seeks to foster critical thinking, stimulate intellectual discourse, and contribute to the ongoing development of legal scholarship. Furthermore, it serves as a bridge between academia and the broader public, encouraging awareness and understanding of key legal concepts and developments.

The deadline for submissions is November 02, 2024. Articles should be submitted to thulawampuocfol@gmail.com
